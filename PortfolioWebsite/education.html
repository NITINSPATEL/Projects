Education

My Institutional BackGround

Grade Nursery - Grade LKG , 2008-2010

My first school, Jindal Public School in Tosham, Bhiwani, Haryana. I remember , I cried a lot when my elder siblings go to school and me at home. This was the time when my parents put me to school having seeing me like so much crying. Spent here the formative years of my schooling.

Grade 11 - Grade 12 , 2020-2022

My 4th, the last one and JEE Time school, KRD International School in Delhi. Mostly went to school to give only exams. Joined this school because my Coaching, Fiitjee South Delhi Branch was also in Delhi. Yeah, so fair enough decision? Kind of, but since it was COVID time, I never went Delhi and always took online classes from my home, Saharanpur, Uttar Pradesh. Yeah so, not completely a good decision.

Grade 1 - Grade 2 , 2010-2012

My second school, Shardein School, Muzaffarnagar, Uttar Pradesh, where I kind of realized my potential , scoring best in class , getting the favored treatment from teachers and all. Made Nibba-Nibbi friendships ;)

Grade 3 - Grade 10 , 2012-2020

The longest school journey happened here in my 3rd school, Pinewood School, Saharanapur, Uttar Pradesh. I think this section is not enough to write in brief all about this school what happened here. Made very good friendships here. The journey of this school will alaways remain closest to my heart, no matter anyone give the recognition or not. Year by year, learnt a lot from this school's journey. My intelligent peer group proved to be very fruitful to me. Spent the best school years here in this school. Love my friends of this school till date, even though I am not in contact with those guys and gals. This is the place where my transition from a carefree child to sensible teen took place. This is the school which taught me almost everything. It will never be enough if I write about my journey of this school. But, Still I have to complete this section, so I would like to conclude this by saying, "Your company of friends plays a major role what kind of person you will be in future."
